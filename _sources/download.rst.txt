.. _bundle-label:

Download
========

Download the latest HyperSpy-bundle installer:

.. button-link:: https://github.com/hyperspy/hyperspy-bundle/releases/latest
    :color: primary
    :shadow:
    :align: center

    :octicon:`desktop-download` Download installer

Depending on your computer, choose the most suitable installer:

.. figure:: _static/bundle_release_download.png
   :width: 100 %
   :target: https://github.com/hyperspy/hyperspy-bundle/releases/latest
   :alt: Links HyperSpy bundle downloads
   :figwidth: 80%

   Select a suitable installer depending on what type of processor is in your computer:
   on Windows and Linux, one of either Intel or AMD, and on Mac, one of either Intel of M1.
